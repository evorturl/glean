# Travel and Conference Policy

Employees must obtain manager approval before booking business travel or conference attendance. International travel should be approved at least two weeks before departure.

Conference travel is reimbursable only when the employee has a defined business purpose such as speaking, customer meetings, or a role-specific training plan.

Employees traveling internationally for business may work remotely during the trip only if the total time working outside their home country remains within the Remote Work Policy limits and Security has approved the travel plan.

Trips longer than seven calendar days require a documented coverage plan and confirmation that Finance and Security have reviewed the itinerary if cross-border work is involved.
