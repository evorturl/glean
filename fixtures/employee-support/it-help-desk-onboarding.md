# IT Help Desk Onboarding Guide

New employees receive a company laptop, security key, and a password manager invitation on their start date. Laptop setup should be completed before accessing production systems.

Multi-factor authentication is required for email, source control, payroll, and VPN access. Employees should contact the IT help desk within one hour if they are unable to enroll in MFA.

Standard IT help desk coverage is Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Pacific Time. High-priority access incidents are targeted for first response within 30 minutes during support hours.

When working remotely, employees must use the company VPN on untrusted networks and should open a help desk ticket before traveling if they expect to work internationally.
