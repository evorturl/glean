# Paid Time Off and Holidays

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off each calendar year in addition to the company holiday schedule.

PTO requests for one to three days should be submitted at least three business days in advance. Requests for four or more consecutive days should be submitted at least two weeks in advance so managers can plan coverage.

Unused PTO may roll over up to five days into the next calendar year. Any balance above five days expires on January 31 unless local law requires a different treatment.

Employees on approved international remote work should not assume PTO approval also covers cross-border work. Remote work abroad still requires the separate approvals listed in the Remote Work Policy.
