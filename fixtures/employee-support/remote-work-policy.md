# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week unless their team has a documented exception approved by People Ops and the department lead.

Employees who want a fully remote arrangement for more than four consecutive weeks must obtain written approval from their manager and submit a workspace safety self-attestation.

International remote work is limited to 15 business days per calendar year. Any request to work from another country requires approval from Legal, Finance, and Security at least two weeks in advance.

Employees must use company-managed devices, connect through the corporate VPN when offsite, and avoid storing customer data locally on personal devices.
