# Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees may expense approved business travel, conference registration, client meals, and office supplies purchased for company use.

Receipts are required for any single expense above 25 USD. Expense reports should be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date through the finance system.

Domestic airfare should be booked in economy class. Lodging should stay within the published city budget unless a director approves an exception in advance.

Business meals are reimbursable when they support travel, recruiting, or customer meetings and include the business purpose plus attendee names.
